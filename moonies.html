How It Works

When you become a Moonie, you’ll be granted access to the Squaremoon platform. This platform is your gateway to a world of opportunities, allowing you to request work, manage hiring projects, contact candidates, and deliver valuable reports for some of the world’s biggest companies.

Why Join Squaremoon?

  • Choose projects that align with your expertise and passion in recruitment and talent engagement.

  • Work on your terms, wherever and whenever you want.

  • Utilise our advanced software to streamline your work, from candidate sourcing to delivering reports.

  • Become part of a community of like-minded professionals, with support from Squaremoon to help you succeed.

  • Start taking on projects in no time with our simple sign-up process.

  • At Squaremoon, your earnings are tied to the impact and quality of your work. We process payments twice a month, ensuring you’re rewarded promptly for your contributions. Whether you’re sourcing top talent or conducting screening calls, you’ll be compensated based on the successful outcomes you deliver.
